TECH

Current battery technology cannot meet the energy and power demands of today's on-the-go consumer. Our silicon-graphene composites enable longer lasting, faster charging batteries.

POINT 1

Current lithium-ion battery technologies, which power portable electronics and electric vehicles, are unable to meet consumer demand for ever increasing range, power, and cycling stability.

POINT 2

Developed and patented by our team collaborating with researchers at Northwestern University and Argonne National Laboratory, SiNode technology utilizes a composite of silicon & graphene in a layered structure.

POINT 3

Our materials deliver higher cell level energy density, with capacities over 2500 mAh/g. Introducing in-plane nano-engineered porosity to graphene layers, allowing rapid ionic diffusion, enables faster charging.
